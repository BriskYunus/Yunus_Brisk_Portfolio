Brisk Yunus

My Name is Brisk Yunus. I was born in Edmonton Alberta, Canada. I was raised here in London Ontario, where I went to school for Interactive Media Design. I studied at Fanshawe College for 2 years, as well as some designing in High School. During my time in School, I wanted to bring peoples ideas to life. I enjoy working with people who have a vision in their heads and need somebody to make it a reality. Whether it is Photography, Cover Arts, Designs, or anything in the creative space, I'm the guy.

Design and Music have always been a passion for me, ever since I was a child. I believe both Design and Music have huge impacts on our lives so I wanted to be a part of that growing industry. I capture the moments of special events through Photography and Images. I have done cover arts for local artist across Canada, including myself. Aside from design, I also do Music Production. Check out all my work in the "PORTFOLIO" section!
